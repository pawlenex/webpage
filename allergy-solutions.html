Protein Allergies

The most common category. Certain proteins trigger immune responses leading to itching, GI distress, and ear infections.

  • Beef (most common in dogs)
  • Chicken
  • Dairy products
  • Fish (common in cats)

Grain Sensitivities

While true grain allergies are less common than believed, some pets do react to specific grains and starches.

  • Wheat & wheat gluten
  • Corn
  • Soy
  • Rice (rare)
Moderate

Additives & Fillers

Artificial colors, preservatives, and low-quality fillers can cause sensitivities that mimic allergic reactions.

  • Artificial colors (Red 40, Yellow 5)
  • BHA / BHT preservatives
  • Carrageenan
  • Propylene glycol
Avoidable
